Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 18, 2026

You can request deletion of data stored by Usludigital 360 for connected Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, WhatsApp, and business workspace accounts.

How To Request Deletion

  1. Email admin@usludigital.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Usludigital 360 Data Deletion Request.
  3. Include the business or company name and the platform account you want deleted.

What We Delete

We delete stored platform connection records, conversation records, comments, messages, analytics snapshots, and related workspace data that can be reasonably matched to the requested account.

Timing

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days after verifying that the requester is authorized to manage the connected business account.

Questions

Contact admin@usludigital.com for questions about deletion or account access.
