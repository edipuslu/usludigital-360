Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Usludigital 360 helps businesses manage social media conversations, analytics, and AI-assisted replies from connected platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and WhatsApp.

Information We Process

When a business connects a platform account, Usludigital 360 may process account identifiers, page or profile metadata, comments, direct messages, mentions, post links, timestamps, and conversation history needed to show the inbox, analytics, and automation tools.

How We Use Information

We use connected platform data only to provide the dashboard features requested by the business: showing inbox items, syncing comments and messages, generating AI reply drafts, posting approved automated replies, and showing performance analytics.

AI Processing

If AI automation is enabled by the business administrator, message and comment content may be sent to the configured AI provider to generate a reply. AI automation can be enabled, scheduled, disabled, or limited by the administrator.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with service providers needed to operate the product, such as hosting, database, email, and AI API providers, or when required by law.

Data Retention

Connected platform data is retained while the business account uses Usludigital 360 or until deletion is requested. Administrators can remove platform connections and request deletion of stored data.

Data Deletion

To request deletion of data connected to Usludigital 360, follow the instructions on our Data Deletion page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact admin@usludigital.com.
